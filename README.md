# Global_sales_performace_report

This is an end-to-end Power BI business intelligence report analyzing global sales performance, operational efficiency, cost structure, and customer behavior across multiple markets, categories, and time periods.

The goal is to demonstrate business-focused analytics — answering why performance differs, where profitability is created or lost, and how customer behavior impacts revenue.

## Business Objectives

The report was designed to answer the following business questions:

How is sales and profitability distributed across markets and categories?

Which product categories and sub-categories drive revenue and profit?

How do shipping cost and delivery time impact operational efficiency?

What is the relationship between discounting and customer loyalty?

How do repeat vs one-time customers contribute to revenue?

Report Structure

The report is organized into four analytical views:

### 1 Executive Summary

- High-level KPIs for quick decision-making:

- Total Sales

- Total Profit

- Total Orders

- YoY Growth %

- Average Discount

- Average Order Value

Purpose: This provide management with a fast snapshot of overall performance.

### 2 Sales Performance

Focuses on revenue generation and sales mix:

- Sales by Market, Category, Sub-Category

- Sales by Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

- Top Countries by Sales

- Avg Sales, Profit & Shipping Cost per Order

Key Insight :

Technology products generate the highest revenue and profit per order, despite higher shipping costs.

### 3 Operations & Cost

Analyzes operational efficiency and cost drivers:

- Average Shipping Cost

- Shipping Cost as % of Revenue

- Average Delivery Time

- On-Time Delivery Rate

- Shipping Cost by Delivery Time Interval

- Profit Margin vs Delivery Time

Key Insight :

Longer delivery times are associated with declining profit margins and higher shipping costs.

### 4 Customer Behavior & Insights

This page examines customer loyalty and purchasing patterns:

- Repeat vs One-Time Customers

- Revenue Contribution by Customer Type

- Repeat Customer Rate

- Discount Effectiveness vs Loyalty

- Product Categories Driving Repeat Revenue

Key Insights:

Repeat customers contribute a disproportionate share of total revenue, indicating strong lifetime value.
